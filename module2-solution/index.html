Chicken

The chicken (Gallus domesticus) is a domesticated species that arose from the red junglefowl, originally from Southeast Asia.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle (Bos taurus). Beef can be prepared in various ways; cuts are often used for steak, which can be cooked to varying degrees of doneness, while trimmings are often ground or minced, as found in most hamburgers.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dishfeaturing specially prepared rice and usually some type of fish or seafood, often raw, but sometimes cooked. And though you might automatically associate the word sushi with raw fish, it's actually the ricethat is the most important ingredient.
